Welcome!

This website is dedicated to promoting inclusive practices in dance education. We believe that dance is for every body, and we aim to create a space where educators, families, and students can learn about adaptive movement strategies, equity, and accessibility in dance.

Why Inclusive Dance?

Inclusive dance allows people of all abilities to experience the joy of movement. By adapting dance instruction to meet diverse needs, we foster creativity, confidence, and community in every dancer.
